# p5-sketches

A collection of visual patches and sketches created with [p5.js](https://p5js.org/).

## Sketches

### Saturn & Witchcraft Collection

Open `index.html` in a browser to view all sketches with a navigation interface.

#### Saturn
A mesmerizing cosmic visualization of Saturn with its iconic rings, orbiting moons, and twinkling stars. Features:
- Realistic ring particle system
- Atmospheric bands on the planet
- Orbiting moons
- Saturn astrological symbols

#### Sigil
A mystical pentagram surrounded by ancient runes, flickering candles, and swirling magical energy. Features:
- Animated pentagram with glowing points
- Rune symbols rotating around the circle
- Flickering candle flames
- Central eye sigil
- Click to conjure bursts of ethereal particles

#### Saturn Occult
The fusion of cosmic and occult - Saturn as an ancient deity. Features:
- Hexagram (Seal of Solomon) symbolism
- Dark energy vortex
- Alchemical sigils (Earth, Saturn, etc.)
- Multiple orbital rings with mystical markers
- Saturn symbol prominently displayed
- Click to create dark energy bursts

## Running the Sketches

1. Open `index.html` in a modern web browser
2. Click on any sketch card to enter the visualization
3. Interact with the sketches by clicking (where supported)

## Technologies

- [p5.js](https://p5js.org/) - JavaScript library for creative coding
